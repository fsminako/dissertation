The Jakarta Composite Index (JCI) closed on a positive note on Thursday, climbing by 0 The Jakarta Composite Index (JCI) closed on a positive note on Thursday, climbing by 0.37 percent to 5,439.83 points. Indonesia Stock Exchange (IDX) data showed that 156 stocks strengthened during the final trading session, while 109 stocks weakened and 111 stocks remained stable, kontan.co.id reported. The volume of trade between the lunchtime and closing sessions involved 5.73 billion stocks worth Rp 5.2 trillion (US$395.2 million). Shares in eight out of 10 sectors traded on the IDX ended in the black by 4 p.m. on Thursday with consumer products recording the highest increase, jumping by 1.62 percent, followed by manufacturing and mining, which rose by 0.79 percent and 0.75 percent, respectively. Only shares in basic industries and agriculture failed to make improvements in the closing trade as they declined by 0.87 percent and 0.31 percent, respectively.
